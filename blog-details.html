Techno-Hub team participating in the ECSA ICT Hackathon 2023

  • Author
  • Oct 31, 2023
  • 1 Comment

The Engineering Council Of South Africa (ECSA) has called upon university students alike to participate in their first hosted hackathon.

The briefing held on the 16th of October 2023 outlined what the hackathon is about and what is expected from the participants, a.k.a "hackers."

Techno-Hub is a trio of students and alumni from Nelson Mandela University participating in the ECSA ICT Hackathon.

Essentially, the hackers are to revamp the old version of the ECSA website, create a new prototype as per technical and desgn specifications. The first presentation was held on the 20th of October 2023. The participation had to be endorsed by the ICT Faculties of the participants' universities.

Why they joined the Hackathon?

This isn't their first rodeo and they enjoy development challenges. It helps inspire them and assist in actual industry growth and personal portfolio's.
